Swahili Pilau

Description

This is a fragrant East African rice dish made with spiced meat, pilau masala, garlic, ginger, and onions. Rich, savory, and perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

Pilau Masala:

  • 1 tsp each: cumin, peppercorns, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom

Main:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 400g beef/goat, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp pilau masala, 1 tsp coriander
  • 3 tbsp oil, salt to taste
  • 4 cups water or broth

Steps

  1. Boil meatwith garlic, ginger, and salt until tender. Keep the broth.
  2. Fry onions until brown. Add garlic, ginger, and spices.
  3. Add meat,tomatoes, and potatoes. Cook briefly.
  4. Stir in rice, then add broth (2:1 liquid to rice). Add salt.
  5. Simmer on lowfor 15â€“20 mins until rice is done. Fluff and serve.
