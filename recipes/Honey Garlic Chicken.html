Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

Crispy bite-sized chicken pieces glazed in a sweet, garlicky honey soy sauce. Great as an appetizer or served with rice.

Ingredients

Chicken:

  • 500g chicken (breast/thigh), cubed
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup flour + 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Oil for frying

Sauce:

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water

Garnish: Sesame seeds, spring onions

Steps

  1. Prep Chicken:Season, dip in egg, coat with flour mix.
  2. Fry:Deep fry until golden, drain on paper towels.
  3. Make Sauce: Simmer honey, soy, garlic, vinegar. Thicken with cornstarch slurry.
  4. Combine: Toss chicken in warm sauce.
  5. Serve: Garnish and enjoy hot.
